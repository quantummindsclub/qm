Contact Us

  • Quantum Minds is a student-driven research and innovation club at the United College of Engineering and Research. We focus on a wide range of technological fields, including space technology, defense, AI, cybersecurity, and more. Our mission is to empower students to explore, innovate, and contribute to real-world technological advancements.

  • To become a member, you need to meet the club's eligibility criteria, which includes academic excellence (minimum GPA of 7.5) and technical proficiency in relevant fields. You can apply through the club's membership portal or get in touch with the club administrators for more details.

  • We focus on several core areas, including: Space Technology, Defense Technology, Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, Research and Innovation, Entrepreneurship and Startups.

  • You can participate in events and workshops by registering on the club's website or through direct invitations. Keep an eye on our Events page for upcoming opportunities and announcements.

  • While prior experience in relevant technical fields can be beneficial, it's not a requirement. We welcome students from all academic backgrounds who are passionate about technology and innovation. Members can learn and develop new skills through hands-on projects, workshops, and collaboration with experts.

  • If you're an organization, faculty, or individual interested in collaborating with Quantum Minds, please visit our Contact Us page to reach out. We are open to partnerships, mentorship, and industry collaborations to enhance our members' learning experiences.
